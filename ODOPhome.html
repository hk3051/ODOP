One District One Product

Welcome To One District One Product Scheme

The main objective of ODOP system is the economic development of each district by focusing on a unique product or industry that has the potential for growth and employment generation. ODOP encourages districts to identify and promote products that are unique to their region.The scheme aims to enhance the skills of local artisans and entrepreneurs involved in the production of the identified products. ODOP seeks to connect local producers with wider markets, both domestic and international.By supporting the growth of local industries and products, ODOP aims to create employment opportunities in rural and semi-urban areas.The initiative emphasizes the sustainable production and marketing of products, taking into consideration environmental and social factors. ODOP promotes balanced regional development by harnessing the unique strengths and potential of each districtOverall, the One District One Product scheme is designed to promote inclusive and sustainable economic growth, preserve traditional craftsmanship, and empower local communities by leveraging their unique products and skills. It aligns with the broader goals of economic development and self-reliance in India.
