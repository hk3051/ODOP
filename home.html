Welcome to ODOP Website


Our "One District One Product" (ODOP) scheme is a initiative in India aimed at boosting rural economies by identifying and promoting one unique product from each district. It seeks to empower local artisans, preserve traditional crafts, and stimulate economic growth by focusing on the production, marketing, and development of these chosen products, thereby fostering regional diversity and self-reliance. This scheme aims to create employment opportunities and improve the income of rural communities while promoting the cultural heritage of each district.It is achive by using method "Supply Chain".

What is Blockchain ?

A blockchain is a distributed database or ledger shared among a computer network's nodes. They are best known for their crucial role in cryptocurrency systems for maintaining a secure and decentralized record of transactions, but they are not limited to cryptocurrency uses.
